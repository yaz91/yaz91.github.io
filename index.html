About Me

I am now a fifth-year Ph.D. student in ECE, Swanson School of Engineering, University of Pittsburgh, supervised by Prof. Heng Huang. My research interests includes graph neural networks, algorithmic fairness and effieicnt training, with applications in medical image, social network, bioinformatics, computer vision, and other relevant data mining problems.
